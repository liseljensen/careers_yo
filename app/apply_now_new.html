FAQs

Do I have to apply online?

In order to be considered for a position posted on our Careers website, you must complete our online application process. This process includes creating a profile and answering job-relevant questions. Plan on at least 20 minutes when you apply.

How do I know that my application has been received?

Upon successful completion of the application process, an email will be sent confirming that your resume was received.

I applied for a job. Now what?

If your skills and qualifications strongly match the position and you are chosen for an interview, a recruiter will contact you regarding next steps in the interview process. It may take several weeks, but you will be contacted via phone or email as to whether you have been chosen for an interview.

How long do jobs stay posted on the website?

Positions will remain posted on our website until they enter the interview stage or have been filled.

I am interested in one of your positions that I saw posted on another website, but I don’t see it on the Simplot Careers website. How do I apply?

When a position is in the interview stage or has been filled, it will no longer appear on our Simplot Careers website. At that time the hiring manager is not accepting any further applications.

Can I change my application after I submit it?

You can make changes and add attachments to your profile, but you cannot make changes to your application after it has been submitted.

What format should my resume be created in?

For best results, please upload your resume as a Microsoft Word or Adobe PDF document.

What should I do if I don’t have an email address?

Click here to create an email address free of charge.

What can I view once I log back into the Career Portal?

You can store your current resume, view your job cart, saved searches and a history of jobs that you have applied for, check the status of your application(s), manage your contact information, including address, phone numbers and email address, and change your password.

What is a “source” and why am I required to select one?

The source lets us know where you saw the position advertised or where you heard about the position. There are two source drop-down menus that you are required to choose from when you apply via our online application system. Please use the first drop-down menu to choose from a list of high-level categories like advertisements, job boards, job fairs, etc. Next, use the second drop-down menu to indicate the specific source, i.e. the names of the advertisement, job board or job fair.

I forgot my Username and Password. How do I retrieve this information?

Your username is the email address you selected when you first registered. If you forgot your password, click the link below the “Login” button and a temporary password will be emailed to you.

I got an error that says “Exception thrown on server.” What does that mean?

Typically that error means that you tried to copy/paste information into your application. Please type the information and that should allow you to proceed.
