About the Program

Overview

Simplot internships are designed to provide meaningful work experiences for students pursuing Bachelors and Master’s Degree programs. All of our internships are paid positions that are offered for a designated period of time. Each posting is unique with the duration and time commitment varied. Interns are assigned specific tasks to accomplish and are expected to participate in a variety of projects based on business group. With these projects, interns have the opportunity to apply the skills and knowledge they are acquiring in school.

Program Highlights

There are many benefits of pursuing an internship with the J.R. Simplot Company. Our interns have the opportunity to attend “Intern Week” which is a summer conference located in Boise, Idaho that includes classroom training, site and field tours, networking, and team building activities. Our goal is to play an integral role in a student’s educational journey by to equipping them with marketable skills and an understanding of each of Simplot’s business groups. We strive to instill a strong desire to work at Simplot after graduation.

Grow Your Career

As one of the world’s largest frozen-potato processors and beef cattle producers, Simplot also plays a major role in the fertilizer manufacturing and retail markets. There are many career opportunities within our diverse company, and an internship experience with Simplot will prove to be beneficial to any student in any discipline. With close to 10,000 employees worldwide, the opportunities are endless. We encourage Interns to explore fulltime career options in all business groups throughout their intern experience.
