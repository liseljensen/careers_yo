Plant Sciences

The J.R. Simplot Company’s Plant Sciences team is the very embodiment of the company’s mission: Bringing Earth’s Resources to Life. Driven by a deep respect for the environment, Simplot has brought together uniquely qualified individuals to face the challenge of feeding the world’s growing population. Finding better ways to grow delicious, safe, affordable and abundant food for people throughout the world is a goal worthy of the effort.

Land & Livestock

Simplot Land & Livestock is the largest beef cattle producer in the western U.S. with extensive farm and ranch holdings, including farms, ranches, and feedlots. Land & Livestock’s feedlot management service includes financing, marketing, veterinary care, transportation, and computerized animal records. The operation also includes a retail division called Simplot Western Stockmen’s that supplies and develops a wide variety of trusted products for a variety of animal health needs.

Animal Sciences

Simplot's Animal Sciences team uses an innovative approach to provide advanced reproductive solutions for beef and dairy cattle producers to improve efficiency, productivity, and profitability in a changing market.

Locations

Headquartered in Boise, Idaho, the J.R. Simplot Company’s major operations are in the U.S., Canada, Mexico, Australia and China with products marketed in more than 40 countries worldwide. Whether processing avocados in Mexico, utilizing plant sciences technology to help grow a healthy potato, or developing agricultural solutions in China, the Simplot Company’s reach is extensive.
